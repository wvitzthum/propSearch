# Swap Rate Source Citation

## Data Used
- **2-Year GBP Swap Rate:** Leading indicator for 2-year fixed mortgage pricing
- **5-Year GBP Swap Rate:** Leading indicator for 5-year fixed mortgage pricing
- **Swap Spread:** Difference between swap rate and BoE base rate

## Source URLs
- **ICE Swap Rate:** https://www.theice.com/marketdata/reports/143
- **Bloomberg:** https://www.bloomberg.com/markets/rates-bonds/government-bonds/uk
- **UK Debt Management Office:** https://www.dmo.gov.uk/

## Methodology Notes
- Swap rates represent the fixed rate at which counterparties would agree to pay floating LIBOR/SONIA
- Mortgage lenders price based on swap rates + margin
- 5-year swap is particularly relevant as it reflects medium-term funding costs
