# Bank of England Source Citation

## Data Used
- **Bank Rate (Base Rate):** Official interest rate set by the Monetary Policy Committee
- **MPC Meeting Dates:** Official schedule of monetary policy meetings
- **Mortgage Rates:** Effective interest rates on new mortgage approvals by LTV band

## Source URLs
- **Main:** https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/
- **Base Rate:** https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/monetary-policy/the-interest-rate-bank-rate
- **MPC Minutes:** https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/monetary-policy/mpc/minutes
- **Statistical Database:** https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/boeapps/database/

## Methodology Notes
- Base rate reflects unanimous or majority MPC vote
- Mortgage rates are weighted averages from regulatory reporting by major lenders
- Data refreshed on or shortly after MPC meeting dates (approximately every 6 weeks)
