# HMRC SDLT Source Citation

## Data Used
- **Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) Rates:** Residential property tax bands
- **First-Time Buyer Relief:** Relief thresholds and rates
- **Additional Property Surcharge:** 3% surcharge for buy-to-let/second homes

## Source URLs
- **Main Guidance:** https://www.gov.uk/stamp-duty-land-tax/residential-property-rates
- **SDLT Calculator:** https://www.tax.service.gov.uk/calculate-stamp-duty-land-tax/

## Methodology Notes
- Rates effective from April 1, 2025
- Thresholds are cumulative (marginal rate system)
- Rates apply to England only (separate regimes for Scotland, Wales, NI)
